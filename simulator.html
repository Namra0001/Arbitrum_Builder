Block Mining Simulator

Experience proof-of-work mining, hashing, and blockchain immutability — hands on.

How it works: Enter data for Block 1 and click "Mine Block" — the simulator increments the nonce until the SHA-256 hash starts with 00 (simulated proof-of-work). Block 2 automatically links to Block 1's hash. Try changing Block 1's data after mining — Block 2 will break, demonstrating chain immutability.
